# Vacation Policy

## Overview

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid vacation per year.

## Request Process

* Submit vacation request via the HR portal
* Requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance
* Manager approval is required

## Extended Leave

* For absences longer than 3 business days:

  * additional approval from department lead is required

## Carry Over

* Up to 5 unused days can be carried over to the next year

## Notes

* HR portal: https://hr.internal.company
